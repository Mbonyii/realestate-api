You've enabled two-factor authentication for your Property Management account. This adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring a verification code in addition to your password when you log in.

Please follow these steps to complete the setup:

  1. Download an authenticator app on your mobile device (such as Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or Authy)
  2. Open the app and scan the QR code below
  3. The app will generate a 6-digit code
  4. Enter this code on the verification page to complete the setup

Important: Keep your authenticator app safe and accessible. You'll need it every time you log in to your account.

If you lose access to your authenticator app, you can reset your two-factor authentication by going through the password recovery process.
